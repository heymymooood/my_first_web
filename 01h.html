What is the HTML?

To understand "HTML" from front to back, let's look at each word that makes up the abbreviation: Hypertext: text (often with embeds such as images, too) that is organized in order to connect related items Markup: a style guide for typesetting anything to be printed in hardcopy or soft copy format Language: a language that a computer system understands and uses to interpret commands.
